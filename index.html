Our mission

A card catalog for the whole internet's free knowledge

Resource Hub started from a simple frustration: the best free tutorials, docs, and courses for any given subject are scattered across a hundred different corners of the web. Finding them usually means ten browser tabs, a dozen search queries, and a lot of second-guessing about which link is actually worth your time.

So we built a catalog. Like the library card catalogs of old, every resource here gets a call number, a category, and a short, honest description — so you can scan a shelf instead of scrolling an algorithmic feed.

What makes the catalog different

  • Frontend-only, by design. There's no server, no account, and nothing to sign up for. Everything runs in your browser, in this single HTML file.
  • Your data stays with you. Bookmarks, search history, and view counts live in your browser's local storage — never on our servers, because we don't have any.
  • Curated, not crawled. Every entry is hand-picked for being genuinely free and genuinely useful, not the highest bidder.
  • Built for scanning. Call numbers, tags, and difficulty badges let you judge a resource before you click through.

How the catalog is organized

Each subject — HTML, Python, Machine Learning, Civil Engineering, and 28 others — lives in its own "drawer" with a dedicated call-number range, visible right on every resource card. Browse by category, search by keyword, or just see what's trending right now.

Something missing?

If there's a resource, category, or feature you'd love to see, we'd genuinely like to hear about it — visit the contact page and send a note.

Privacy Policy

Resource Hub is a frontend-only application — this entire site is one HTML file. There is no backend server, no database, and no account system, which means there is very little of your data for us to collect in the first place.

What stays on your device

  • Bookmarks — the list of resources you've saved.
  • Search history — your most recent search terms (capped at 20).
  • View & search counters — used to power the Trending page for you.
  • Theme preference — whether you last chose light or dark mode.

All of the above is stored using your browser's local storage, scoped to this file. It never leaves your device, is never transmitted to any server, and is fully under your control — clear it at any time from the Bookmarks page or your browser settings.

What we don't do

  • We don't use cookies for tracking.
  • We don't run analytics scripts that identify you personally.
  • We don't require sign-up, login, or an email address to use any feature.
  • We don't sell or share data, because we don't collect any to begin with.

Third-party links

Resource cards link out to third-party websites. Once you leave Resource Hub, that site's own privacy policy applies.

Content delivery networks

This page loads fonts, icons, and JavaScript libraries from public CDNs so it looks and works well. These providers may log basic request data like your IP address as part of standard web infrastructure, independent of anything Resource Hub itself does.
